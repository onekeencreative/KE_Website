Why Use Us

  • It's Easy

    We connect directly to your system, so there is no complicated submission process. Just send a request via email and receive an estimate in return. Without the overhead of gathering documention, your team can focus on things that matter.

  • We're Experts

    Our expert estimators understand not only the software, but the business of construction. We identify work that is really needed so that your estimates are fair and accurate.

  • Improve Your Margins

    With quality estimates, you have clear scope and the budget you need to run a project. Well executed projects mean margins improve and revenue increases.

  • It's Cost Effective

    Having employees is expensive and they cost you even when work is slow. With Keen Estimates, you only pay for us when you need us. With the average estimate at .06% of your project, your business overhead will decrease.

The Keen Team

Craig Keen

Estimating Lead

Craig has over 20 years of experience in the industry and is Level 3 Xactimate and IICRC certified. His dedication and expertise ensures every estimate written meets our high standards. When he’s not writing estimates, he likes to write code and develop solutions to support the business.

Dani Keen

Business Lead

Dani has a degree Management Information Systems and over a decade of estimating experience in other industries. Her focus on process improvement allows Keen Estimates to continually grow to serve our customers. When she’s not working, she likes to write and roller-skate by the lake.
